Personal Journey

I believe it's never too late to chase a new dream - even if it means changing careers at the age of 28. My journey into web development started with a bold step: enrolling in an online bootcamp for full-stack development. But learning a skill this complex takes time, patience, and persistence. I quickly realized that mastering the craft wouldn't happen overnight, and that every step is part of the process.

Right now, I'm focusing on front-end development, shaping digital experiences one element at a time. Through freeCodeCamp, I started my deep dive into responsive web design using HTML and CSS. Soon, I'll be expanding my expertise into JavaScript and frameworks like Bootstrap. With every lesson, I absorb more of the syllabus, strengthening my skills bit by bit.

Of course setbacks happen. Frustration, overwhelm, and moments of doubt are part of the journey. But no matter how many times I stumble, I remind myself of my ultimate goal: becoming a full-stack developer. Growth is not linear - it's full of twists and turns, but every challenge refines my skills and strengthens my resilience.

So I keep moving forward. Setbacks? Frustrations? They're simply part of learning. Through it all, I rely on my greatest strength - my willpower.
